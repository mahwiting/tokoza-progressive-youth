About Us

Learn more about our organization, mission, and team

Our Story

Thokoza Progressive Youth was founded in 2015 by a group of young community activists who recognized the need for youth empowerment programs in the Thokoza area. What started as a small initiative has grown into a thriving organization that impacts hundreds of young lives each year.

Our Mission

To empower young people through education, skills development, and community engagement, enabling them to become productive members of society and leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

A community where all youth have access to opportunities that enable them to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
