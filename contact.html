Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join your programs?

You can join our programs by visiting our center during operating hours to register, or by filling out the online registration form on our website. Some programs may have specific requirements or application processes.

Do I need to pay to participate?

Most of our programs are free for youth participants, thanks to funding from donors and sponsors. Some specialized training programs may have a small fee, but we offer scholarships for those who can't afford it.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities. Please fill out the contact form indicating your interest in volunteering, or visit our center to discuss how your skills can contribute to our programs.

What age groups do you serve?

Our programs primarily serve youth aged 18-35, though some community events are open to all ages. Specific programs may have age requirements listed in their descriptions.

About Us

Thokoza Progressive Youth is a community-based organization dedicated to empowering young people through education, skills development, and social engagement.
